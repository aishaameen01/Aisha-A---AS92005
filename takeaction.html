Reduce Paper/Wood Consumption

  • Go digital – use emails and e-books instead of printing.
  • Print double-sided when needed.
  • Reuse scrap paper for notes or lists.
  • Avoid disposable wooden cutlery – use reusable utensils.
  • Buy recycled paper products.
  • Unsubscribe from junk mail.
  • Use cloth instead of paper towels.
  • Borrow books or tools instead of buying new ones.
  • Choose digital billing and receipts.

Support Eco-Friendly Products

Eco-friendly products help reduce waste, save energy, and protect the environment. They are better choices for a cleaner, greener planet.

  • Made from natural or recycled materials
  • Biodegradable or compostable
  • Non-toxic and safe to use
  • Reusable and long-lasting
  • Low carbon footprint
  • Supports sustainability

Examples: Bamboo toothbrushes, cloth bags, beeswax wraps, reusable bottles, bar soap, recycled notebooks.

Volunteer Oppurtunities

  • 🌳 Plant a tree – even one tree makes a difference!
  • 🤝 Volunteer for local clean-up or tree planting events.
  • 🛒 Buy eco-friendly products and support green businesses.
  • 📚 Learn and share information about deforestation and kaitiakitanga.
  • 📱 Use social media to raise awareness and inspire others.
  • 📝 Write to leaders and ask for stronger environmental protection laws.
