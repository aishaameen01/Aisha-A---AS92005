CAUSES OF DEFORESTATION

Logging

Cutting down trees for timber reduces forest cover and affects biodiversity.

Agriculture Expansion

Forests are cleared to make space for crops and livestock farming.

Urban Development

Growing cities often replace forests with buildings, roads, and infrastructure.

Mining

Mining operations destroy large areas of forest and pollute ecosystems.

Forest Fires

Both natural and man-made fires destroy millions of hectares of forest each year.

Did You Know?

  • We lose 10 million hectares of forest every year.
  • Deforestation contributes about 10% of global warming emissions.
  • Over 80% of land animals and plants live in forests.
